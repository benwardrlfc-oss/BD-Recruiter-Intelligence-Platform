Update 4 – Product Improvements and Feature Adjustments

This update contains several UI, UX, and functional improvements that need to be implemented across the platform.

The goal is to ensure the product feels clean, premium, and highly actionable, with filters and personalization that reflect the user's configured market settings.

1. Homepage – Market Signals Section

Currently there are two “Scan Market Signals” modules on the homepage.

This is unnecessary and creates duplication.

Changes required

Remove the top Scan Market Signals component entirely.

Keep only the main primary Scan Market Signals section on the page.

The homepage should feel cleaner and less repetitive.

2. Market Scope – Dynamic Personalisation

The Market Scope panel must become fully dynamic and automatically reflect the user's settings.

Currently the interface includes an “Edit Scope” button, which is no longer necessary.

Changes required

Remove the Edit Scope button.

The Market Scope panel should automatically update based on:

Geography

Sector

Functional focus

Seniority level

Any additional filters configured in Settings.

The Market Scope panel should act as a live reflection of the user’s configured market coverage, not something edited directly on the homepage.

3. Venture Intelligence – Filter System

The current filter system is not specific enough and needs to be simplified.

The filters should only include three categories:

Fund Size

Deployed Capital

Engagement

These filters must function properly so users can sort signals:

Top-down

Bottom-up

This allows users to quickly identify:

major active investors

emerging funds

highly active capital deployment patterns.

4. Content Studio – Signal Selection and Writing Controls

The Content Studio must provide more control when generating posts.

Signal selection improvements

Users must be able to:

Select specific signal types

Search for specific news articles

Choose the signal they want to create content from

Writing controls

Users must be able to control:

Tone of voice

professional

conversational

analytical

bold/thought leadership

Post length

short

medium

long

This should not be automatic. The user should explicitly select the length.

The system should generate content based on:

selected signal

selected tone

selected length

user market settings.

5. Market Intelligence – Upcoming Hires

The Upcoming Hires predictions need to be significantly more intelligent.

Each predicted hire must include:

The role predicted

A clear explanation of why this role is expected

Supporting signals

Examples of similar companies hiring this role

Example output structure:

Predicted Role: Chief Medical Officer
Reason: Recent Series B funding combined with upcoming clinical program expansion
Comparable companies hiring this role: Company A, Company B, Company C

This makes the intelligence actionable for recruiters.

6. Market Intelligence – Signal Linking

All signals displayed on this page must be clickable and linked to the appropriate location, such as:

company profile page

original news source

related signal cluster.

This prevents dead information and allows users to explore deeper.

7. Geographic Hotspots – Interactive Map

The Geographic Hotspots section should become fully interactive.

Required functionality:

Clicking the hotspot opens a world map heatmap

Users can pan and zoom

Companies appear as data points

When hovering over a company:

show company name

show location

show key signal summary

When clicking the company:

navigate directly to the Company Profile page.

This should feel similar to a venture ecosystem heatmap.

8. BD Script Generator – Signal-Based Personalisation

The BD Script generator must produce highly contextual scripts based on the selected signal.

Opportunity selection filters

Users must be able to filter opportunities by:

Hiring

Funding

Approval

Partnership

Acquisition

Other major signals

There must also be a search bar to search news or companies.

Script Personalisation

Scripts must automatically incorporate the user's settings, such as:

Example:

If a user is configured as:

Diagnostics recruiter
USA market
Commercial roles

The script should reflect that context.

Script tone and format

Scripts should be formatted in a way that works well for SourceWhale outreach.

Example structure:

Good morning {{First Name}}

I noticed your recent [signal].

Many companies in the [sector] space are now beginning to expand their [function] leadership following this type of milestone…

9. Homepage Banner Metrics

The top homepage banner includes:

Signals Today

High Probability

These numbers must become clickable.

Clicking them should navigate to the relevant filtered signal view showing the items behind that metric.

10. Light Mode Visual Improvements

The Light Mode colour scheme currently has several readability issues.

Problems include:

extremely bright elements

blue text on blue backgrounds

poor contrast in certain sections

Scan Market Signals panel being too bright

Changes required

Adjust colour palette to maintain high contrast

Ensure all text is readable

Keep the interface clean, modern, and professional

The design must feel like a premium intelligence platform, not a bright consumer UI.

11. Targets Page → Rename to Companies

The Targets section should be renamed to “Companies.”

Page structure

The page should contain two sections.

Section 1 – Top 10 Accounts

The top of the page should display:

Top 10 companies

Each company should include:

key signals

reason it is ranked highly

hiring probability

recent news.

Section 2 – Full Company List

Below the Top 10 accounts there should be a complete list of all companies in the user's market.

Users should be able to click any company to view:

latest signals

recent news

predicted hiring needs

funding history.

Filtering options

Users must be able to filter companies by:

location

funding stage

alphabetical order.

12. Settings – Geography Structure

The geography selector needs to be much more detailed.

USA

Selecting USA should expand into all US states.

Europe

Selecting EU should expand into all countries.

APAC

Selecting APAC should expand into all countries.

This allows much more precise market targeting.

13. Settings – Sector and Subsector Structure

The sector system must be structured properly.

Primary Sector

Examples:

Biotechnology

CRO

Pharma

Diagnostics

MedTech

Secondary Sector

Once a primary sector is selected, a secondary sector menu should appear.

Examples:

Biotechnology
→ Cell & Gene Therapy
→ Oncology
→ Immunology
→ Synthetic Biology

CRO
→ Preclinical
→ Clinical

The system must not mix modalities, therapeutic areas, and sectors incorrectly.

14. Settings – Seniority Level

Users must be able to define the level they recruit.

Add options such as:

C-Suite

VP

Director

Manager

Entry level

15. Settings – Functional Focus

The functional focus needs to be expanded to better reflect life science roles.

Examples include:

R&D

Translational Science

Clinical Development

Regulatory Affairs

Quality

Commercial

Market Access

Manufacturing

CMC

This ensures signals and predictions are relevant to the user’s recruiting niche.

Final Goal

After these changes the platform should feel like:

a high-end market intelligence system

personalised to each recruiter’s niche

focused on actionable signals

extremely clean and easy to navigate.

The interface should prioritise:

clarity
relevance
precision
professional presentation.
