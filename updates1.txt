Implement the following updates to improve the platform’s usability, intelligence output, and alignment with its purpose as a BD intelligence and action engine for executive search and market intelligence.

The goal is to ensure the system focuses on:

market intelligence

hiring signals

venture activity

strategic BD opportunities

actionable insights

The platform must not function as a candidate database or CRM. It should instead act as a market intelligence terminal that surfaces signals and enables strategic BD actions.

1. Introduce a Market Scope / Market Focus System

The platform currently lacks a clear definition of the market it is analysing. This must be added as a core feature.

Add a Market Scope panel at the top of the BD Command Centre that defines the context the system is analysing.

Example structure:

Market Scope

Sector: Biotech
Subsector / Therapeutic Area: Oncology
Geography: USA → Massachusetts → Boston
Company Stage: Seed – Series B
Function Focus: R&D Leadership / C-Suite

Include an Edit Market Scope button allowing the user to modify these parameters.

The Market Scope should influence and filter the entire platform:

hiring signals

venture intelligence

trends

company insights

live roles

signal detection

candidate matching analysis

This ensures all intelligence is contextual and market-specific rather than generic.
