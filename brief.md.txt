Build a production-ready SaaS web application called “BD Intelligence OS for Recruiters”.

This is a pure business development intelligence platform for recruiters and executive search consultants. It is NOT a CRM, ATS, or candidate management system. Its only purpose is to help recruiters identify who to target, why they matter now, what angle to use, and what content to publish so they generate more BD conversations, more relevance in outreach, more inbound leads, and ultimately more vacancies.

CORE PRODUCT PURPOSE

The platform must ingest market intelligence daily, analyse it with AI, and turn it into actionable BD opportunities for recruiters working in specific niches such as:
- Early Stage Biotech USA
- Venture Capital Biotech Europe
- Diagnostics US
- MedTech Europe
- CRO/CDMO Global
- Oncology Clinical Biotech USA
- ADC Biotech Europe
- Synthetic Biology USA

The platform must be highly personalised. Every user configures their own market focus so the system becomes a tailored BD intelligence environment for that user.

The platform must answer these questions every day:
1. What happened in my market?
2. Which companies or investors should I target?
3. Why are they relevant now?
4. What hiring implication or growth signal does this create?
5. What is the best stakeholder type to approach?
6. What should I say in outreach?
7. What content can I publish from these signals?
8. Which companies are relevant for a candidate I want to monetise?

SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

Build the platform using:
- Frontend web app with modern SaaS UI
- Backend APIs and database
- AI agent layer
- Daily automated market intelligence ingestion layer
- Scoring and rules engine
- Personalised user configuration layer

The platform must use AI agents, not just simple prompts.

AI AGENT REQUIREMENTS

Build multiple specialised AI agents that work together:

1. Market Intelligence Agent
Purpose:
Scour the web daily for relevant news, market updates, funding announcements, leadership changes, hiring signals, partnerships, M&A, IPOs, clinical milestones and investor activity.
It must gather relevant information for each user’s configured market focus.

2. Signal Classification Agent
Purpose:
Take raw articles and classify them into structured signal types such as:
- Funding
- Partnership
- Leadership Change
- Hiring Signal
- Clinical Milestone
- Acquisition / M&A
- IPO / Financing
- VC Portfolio Activity
It must tag geography, sector, stage, modality, and functions impacted.

3. Opportunity Intelligence Agent
Purpose:
Translate each signal into BD implications.
For each signal it must generate:
- why this matters
- likely hiring implication
- likely business development relevance
- recommended stakeholder role to target
- best outreach angle

4. Opportunity Scoring Agent
Purpose:
Score every company and investor by BD value.
Scoring must consider:
- signal strength
- signal recency
- company lifecycle stage
- hiring intensity
- signal density
- momentum
- investor backing

5. Candidate Monetisation Agent
Purpose:
When a recruiter uploads a CV or candidate summary, analyse the candidate and return:
- best-fit target companies
- why they fit commercially
- which stakeholder to target
- outreach angle
- prebuilt e-shot / outreach draft

6. BD Script Generator Agent
Purpose:
Generate outreach messaging from any opportunity.
It must generate:
- email opener
- LinkedIn opener
- cold call opener
- short BD angle summary

7. Content Agent
Purpose:
Generate LinkedIn posts, newsletter ideas, and market commentary from recent signals, trend clusters, or venture activity.

All agents must work from structured data and user-specific market filters.

MARKET PERSONALISATION

Each user must configure a personalised market profile.

Required setup fields:
- Geography
- Sector
- Sub-sector / indication area
- Company stage
- Function focus (R&D, Clinical, Commercial, CMC, Regulatory, Operations, Manufacturing, Medical Affairs etc.)
- Optional modality / technology focus
- Optional investor focus

Examples:
- Early Stage Biotech, USA, R&D + Clinical
- Venture Capital Biotech, Europe
- Diagnostics, US, Commercial
- CRO/CDMO, Global, Manufacturing
- Oncology Biotech, Europe, Clinical

Users must be able to save multiple market profiles and switch between them.
All intelligence, dashboards, scores, trends, signals, target accounts, live roles and content must recalculate based on the active user market profile.

DAILY INTELLIGENCE INGESTION

The system must scour the web daily and automatically ingest relevant information.

Requirements:
- Run daily intelligence collection jobs
- Search relevant market sources
- Parse relevant pages/articles
- Deduplicate signals
- Score article/source relevance
- Store source URL and source name
- Link each signal to relevant company/investor entities
- Ignore irrelevant generic news

The system must support a broad source architecture so the intelligence layer can check:
- industry news sites
- biotech / medtech / diagnostics media
- company news pages
- press releases
- VC websites / portfolio updates
- public job boards / company hiring pages
- regulatory / clinical milestone sources where relevant

2. BD Command Centre Dashboard

Must show:
- top opportunities
- latest relevant signals
- funding and investor activity
- live hiring signals
- high momentum companies
- trend snapshots

3. Market Radar
A searchable, filterable intelligence feed.
Each item must show:
- source
- summary
- why it matters
- BD angle
- company/investor
- signal type
- impacted function
- relevant market tags

Rank all companies/investors using a visible score.

5. Momentum Detection

6. BD Timing Window

- Contact Now

- score
- momentum
- timing window

- outreach angle

8. Opportunity Timeline

9. Similar Company & Competitor Discovery

11. Candidate Matcher

12. Live Hiring Signals

13. BD Script Generator
Generate outreach copy for email, LinkedIn and calls using signal context.

14. Content Studio
Generate:
- LinkedIn posts

15. Market Trend & Momentum Insights

UI requirements:

- strong filtering system
- searchable global search

- mobile responsive but desktop-first

Every key record view must contain an Action Panel showing:

- Recommended next action
- Recommended stakeholder to approach
- Outreach angle
- Opportunity score
- Timing window

FUNCTION-SPECIFIC PERSONALISATION

The platform must tailor outputs based on the user’s selected function focus.

Examples:
If user focuses on Clinical:
- prioritise companies with clinical milestones, clinical hiring, CMO / VP Clinical relevance

If user focuses on R&D:
- prioritise platform companies, CSO hires, discovery/preclinical signals, scientific leadership relevance

If user focuses on Commercial:
- prioritise product launches, market expansion, commercial hiring, VP Sales / GM / Commercial Director relevance

If user focuses on CMC / Manufacturing:
- prioritise manufacturing partnerships, scale-up signals, plant expansion, CMC leadership roles

This functional tailoring must affect:
- signals shown
- opportunity score
- hiring need inference
- stakeholder recommendations
- script generation
- content ideas

IMPORTANT PRODUCT CONSTRAINTS

Do NOT build this as:
- a CRM
- a candidate pipeline
- an ATS
- a contact database
- a generic news reader

Keep the product focused on BD intelligence, opportunity prioritisation, and actionable outreach preparation.

FINAL STANDARD

The product should feel like a recruiter’s daily BD operating system.

A user should be able to log in and immediately understand:
- what changed in their market
- who is worth targeting
- why those accounts matter now
- what the likely hiring need is
- what they should say in outreach
- what content they can publish today

Make sensible engineering decisions and continue building unless something is critically ambiguous.
